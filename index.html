Covid-19 EDA Project


In this exploratory data analysis project, I delve into multiple csv files to analyze Covid-19 data in India. Using python, specifically Numpy, Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib, I analyzed the recovery/mortality rate and the amount of active cases per state. I built visualizations to showcase the growth trend among active cases and show relationships between vaccination and mortality rates.
